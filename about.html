--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About Having grown up before the internet, it took a rather long time for me to discover how much I enjoy coding. It started a little over a year ago during a conversation with some old friends who started a tech company. "What if we payed for you to learn iOS development so you could come work for us?" That sounded good to me! I began investigating immediately, and although their idea never panned out, I was hooked from the first tutorial. HTML? CSS? This is so cool! After a year of independent study, I signed up for a 12-week "Back-End Engineering" course at The Iron Yard here in Nashville. And now... here I am. Ready to roll. When I'm not coding, I'm probably making music on something (piano/guitar/drums), working on a foreign language (Spanish, French, German, Russian), or doing whatever it is my five year old son has instructed me to do, which very likely has something to do with ninjas. ### Skills Ruby, Rails, Python, Flask, JavaScript, AngularJS, HTML/CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, Bourbon, Materialize, SQL, RSpec, Git, Heroku, AWS S3
